# Decentralized Logistics and Freight Management

## Overview

This project implements a blockchain-based solution for logistics and freight management, creating a transparent, efficient, and secure platform for global shipping operations. By leveraging distributed ledger technology, the system reduces fraud, streamlines payments, enhances visibility, and builds trust between all participants in the supply chain.

## Key Components

### Carrier Verification Contract
Validates and maintains a registry of legitimate transportation companies, including their credentials, insurance status, safety records, and operational capacity. This creates a trusted network of verified carriers and reduces the risk of fraudulent actors in the logistics ecosystem.

### Shipment Booking Contract
Manages cargo space reservations, allowing shippers to secure capacity directly with carriers through smart contracts. The system automates rate negotiations, booking confirmations, and documentation, creating an immutable record of agreed terms and conditions.

### Route Tracking Contract
Monitors the movement of goods during transit using IoT integration and authenticated checkpoints. Each shipment's location, condition, and handling events are recorded on the blockchain, providing real-time visibility and creating an auditable chain of custody.

### Payment Escrow Contract
Holds and releases funds based on predefined delivery conditions and confirmation events. The contract automatically releases payment to carriers once delivery is verified, reducing payment disputes and improving cash flow for transportation providers.

## Benefits

- **Reduced Fraud**: Verified carrier network prevents fraudulent shipping arrangements
- **Enhanced Visibility**: Real-time tracking of shipments throughout the entire logistics journey
- **Streamlined Payments**: Automatic payment release upon delivery confirmation
- **Dispute Resolution**: Immutable records of agreements and delivery events
- **Operational Efficiency**: Automated documentation and verification processes
- **Trust Building**: Transparent rating system for carriers based on verified performance
